Good to Know

Honest
answers.

We'd rather you know now than be surprised later. Here's everything worth knowing before you book.

  • When is peak season and how does booking work?
    Peak season runs June 15 – September 1. During this time The Tara books Saturday to Saturday, 7-night minimum. Returning guests get first access in the fall before we open the public calendar. Shoulder season (May, early June, September–October) has more flexible minimums — ask us.
  • The listing shows one bathroom — is that okay for 8 guests?
    Fair question, and we appreciate you asking. The honest answer: yes, and here's why it works. The three bedrooms are spread across the house and guests naturally settle into a rhythm. It has never been cited as an issue in any review across three years of hosting. That said, we believe in full transparency — if two bathrooms is a hard requirement for your group, we'd rather you know that upfront.
  • Are pets really welcome?
    Yes, genuinely. Dogs are welcome at The Tara — the yard, the riverfront, and the neighborhood are great for them. Just let us know when you book so we can be ready.
  • Can we use the dock and water equipment?
    Absolutely. The dock accommodates boats and we have kayaks, canoes, and paddleboards for guest use. The Intermediate River connects to the Southern Chain of Lakes including Lake Bellaire and Torch Lake. Some guests kayak all the way to Torch from the dock.
  • What are check-in and check-out times?
    Check-in is after 3:00 PM, check-out by 11:00 AM. Self check-in via keypad — no need to coordinate arrivals. During peak season, Saturday-to-Saturday stays are standard.
  • How do I reach Dave & Anne directly?
    Call or text 231.431.0041, or email us at our email. We respond within a few hours.
